Lifetime Value (LTV)

How much revenue a customer generates over their entire lifetime.

How much it costs to acquire one new customer.

Your predictable monthly revenue and annualized run rate.

Cash Runway

How many months until your cash runs out at current burn rate.

CAC Payback Period

Months to recoup customer acquisition cost from gross profit.

Churn Rate

Percentage of customers lost over a given period, normalized to monthly and annual rates.

SaaS Quick Ratio

Ratio of revenue gains to losses. Above 4 = healthy growth engine.

Net Dollar Retention (NDR)

Revenue retained from existing customers including expansion. Above 110% = strong.

Rule of 40

Revenue growth rate + profit margin should exceed 40% for a healthy SaaS.
